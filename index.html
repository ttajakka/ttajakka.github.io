Research

My research focuses on algebraic geometry. In particular, I'm interested in moduli spaces of sheaves and complexes on varieties.

My advisor is Jarod Alper.

  • Uhlenbeck Compactification as a Bridgeland Moduli Space
    In this paper I prove that certain Bridgeland moduli spaces on surfaces are projective, and compare these moduli spaces to the Uhlenbeck compactification.
  • Seshadri's First Main Lemma
    In this short note, I strengthen the conclusion and simplify the proof of the "First Main Lemma" in Seshadri's paper Vector Bundles on Curves.
